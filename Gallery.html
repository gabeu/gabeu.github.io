Multiplicative functions

Plots connected to multiplicative functions, prime patterns, and a Sierpinski-triangle style construction.

By setting \(f(p_n^k)=2^{2^{-n}}\), where \(p_n\) is the \(n\)th prime, and extending multiplicatively, one gets a striking Sierpinski-triangle style construction. The twin-prime version above still exhibits the same pattern. A useful starting hint appears here.
